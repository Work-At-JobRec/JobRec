# Development Methodology
Our team has chosen Scrum as the development methodology for the JobRec system. Scrum is an Agile framework that organizes development into short, time-boxed iterations called sprints, allowing teams to continuously inspect progress and adapt based on feedback.
Scrum is well-suited for JobRec because the project involves multiple interdependent components, including resume parsing, job scraping, backend APIs, database integration, and frontend user flows. These components must be integrated incrementally, and their performance, especially the AI-based recommendation quality, cannot be fully predicted upfront. A sprint-based structure allows the team to deliver working increments, validate recommendation accuracy, and refine system behavior after each iteration rather than waiting until the end of the project. Additionally, Scrum aligns well with the course structure by enabling regular demonstrations and progress reviews. While some tasks, such as scraper maintenance or debugging external APIs, may be interrupt-driven, this will be mitigated by reserving sprint capacity for unplanned work and continuously reprioritizing the product backlog.
